Interview Preparation

Teamleader Electrical Utilities · Ciner Glass, Lommel, Belgium

Opening line (honest): “I am an electrician in Electrical Utilities at Ciner Glass in Lommel since March. I work on site as a technician. I apply for teamleader because I previously led 30+ electricians at COMEX for eight years, and I know this site from daily work.”

Documents to bring / present

  • Working Method — support for independent electricians
  • Utilities & Site Scope — PC-10/20, UPS, generator, LPG, SCADA
  • KPI method — statistical improvement, charts
  • Leadership advantages — vs typical applicant
  • Team development — supervision, matrix, multicultural (main story)

Multicultural & language (honest)

  • Team: Dutch, Turkish background, EU staff on English, Brazil, others — informal cultural circles exist.
  • Your offer: English C1 leadership; Dutch beginner (course just started) — transparent, with a clear NL development plan for yourself and the team.
  • Mixed mentor pairs + English technical docs + Dutch for official/site compliance.
  • Do not promise fluent Dutch immediately — promise structure, competence growth, COMEX-scale leadership.

What to emphasize

  • COMEX — 30+ people, mostly experienced electricians; planning, coaching, KPI, subcontractors.
  • Support model — field teams need structure from teamleader, not more pressure without backup.
  • Holistic utilities — whole power path + specialist systems (see scope doc).
  • KPI discipline — measure → pattern → action → verify (see KPI doc).
  • Ciner Glass today — floor knowledge as electrician (without overstating authority).

What not to claim

  • You do not currently coordinate contractors or decide priorities.
  • You are not a maintenance engineer today — you are an electrician.
  • Do not describe your current electrician role using COMEX Head of Service responsibilities.

Questions to ask

  • Expected split between site supervision and full team leadership — timeline?
  • Initial team size and ratio internal vs external electricians?
  • How will the Electrical Utilities team be structured during site phase vs production?
  • CMMS, reporting, and KPI expectations for the teamleader?
