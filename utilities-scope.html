Utilities & Site Scope

Complexity at Lommel — why the teamleader must see the whole system

Łukasz Klimowski · Electrician, Electrical Utilities | Ciner Glass, Lommel, Belgium

Electrical Utilities is responsible for reliable power supply and for supporting specialist installations across a large, multi-building site. This map reflects my current field understanding — useful for showing I already grasp the scale of the role.

Site zones (buildings & areas)

Figure 1 — Main zones where utilities work is performed (not to scale).

PC-10 Production complex — existing phase; extensive electrical infrastructure
PC-20 New production building — commissioning and growth area
Utility building Central utilities hub — distribution, panels, critical feeds
Batch House Batch-related power and process utilities
SCADA pump assembly / related areas Specialist equipment — coordination with automation interfaces
Automatic service warehouse Low-oxygen environment — special safety and supply requirements
Site-wide routes Large footprint — cable routes, trenches, external feeds
Specialist partner zones Utilities supports power to specialist subcontractors/systems
